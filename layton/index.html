Professor Layton and the
London Holiday
London Holiday is a Japan-exclusive prologue to Diabolical Box 🇺🇸 / Pandora’s Box 🇪🇺.
It was present alongside the Japanese versions of the main game, both in the original DS version and in the HD mobile re-release.

The transcript is now complete and available in the form of an interactive walkthrough of sorts, in which you can sort of navigate as if you played the game yourself. The Japanese text has been roughly translated a first time, but a localised English fan-translation is still in the works for a more casual read.
Inspector Chelmey’s Casebook
Special Recipe
Special Recipe is a Japan-exclusive DoCoMo game featuring Chelmey and Barton as protagonists. It contains two parts, which had to be downloaded as two separate apps at a time when the online service was still available (prior to 2014).
A full playthrough has been found by some miracle, making this transcript possible, but this game is still considered lost media due to the fact that getting your hands on this game legally is now impossible.

The Japanese text has been roughly translated a first time, but a localised English fan-translation is planned in the future for a more casual read.
Professor Layton and the
Mansion of the
Deathly Mirror
Mansion of the Deathly Mirror is a Japan-exclusive DoCoMo game with an exclusive story which was about as long as a full game from the main series. It contains six chapters, which had to be downloaded as six separate apps back when the online service was still available (prior to 2014). It is now considered lost media.

This transcript has started but is far from complete, and quite experimental for now. Since we do not have access to the full game, let’s be patient and hope for the best!
Professor Layton and the
Wandering Castle
Wandering Castle is the first Japan-exclusive light novel to be released.
Its story is set in the autumn between Diabolical Box 🇺🇸 / Pandora’s Box 🇪🇺 and Unwound Future 🇺🇸 / Lost Future 🇪🇺.
Professor Layton and the
Phantom Deity
Phantom Deity is the second Japan-exclusive light novel to be released.
Its story is set when Luke was ten years old, during summer, and spans multiple weeks. It is set after Eternal Diva, which Luke states to have been his first adventure with Professor Layton (aside from the one in Misthallery).
Professor Layton and the
Illusory Forest
Illusory Forest is the third Japan-exclusive light novel to be released. It is set on the same year as Miracle Mask, though it is unclear for now whether it is set before or after it.
Professor Layton and the
Eternal Diva (novel)
The Eternal Diva novel is a Japan-exclusive novellisation of the movie of the same name, narrated by both Luke and Janice/Melina.
It contains some exclusive scenes that were absent from the movie.

A more casual, less accurate but more enjoyable fan-translation is available and posted chapter by chapter, weekly, on the @lutiaslayton Tumblr blog. The link is provided if you open the Navigation pane and scroll to the bottom.

Do keep in mind that, unfortunately, these translations are not the most faithful to the originals. They do the job if you want to know what the stories are about, but they are not accurate and at times either make mistakes, or completely invents aspects that were absent from the original novels.
