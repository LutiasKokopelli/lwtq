Professor Layton and the
London Holiday
London Holiday is a Japan-exclusive prologue to Diabolical Box 🇺🇸 / Pandora’s Box 🇪🇺
It was released alongside the main game, both in the original DS version and in the HD mobile re-release.
Inspector Chelmey’s Casebook
Special Recipe
Special Recipe is a Japan-exclusive DoCoMo game featuring Chelmey and Barton as protagonists.

This website, sadly, does not cover every single Layton media in existence. For as exhaustive a list as possible, you can take a look at:

  • The thorough research displayed in this Reddit post, written and maintained by u/SelketTheOrphan.
  • The German Wiki, which is far more complete than the English one; the caveat here being the language.
  • This spreadsheet, which attempts to keep track of what has been transcribed/scanned, and what has yet to be fully documented. Please contact me (@lutiaslayton on tumblr or @lutiaskokopelli on Discord) if you find mistakes, duplicates, or missing entries!

My transcription and translation of the main three novels is far from complete for the time being; but they have also been translated years ago by someone else, and these translations can be read on Google Docs:

  • Professor Layton and the Wandering Castle
  • Professor Layton and the Phantom Deity
  • Professor Layton and the Illusory Forest

Do keep in mind that, unfortunately, these translations are not the most faithful to the originals. They do the job if you want to know what the stories are about, but they are not accurate and at times either make mistakes, or completely invents aspects that were absent from the original novels.
