◆ Main Characters ◆

Professor Layton… Full name Hershel Layton. He is a university professor who specializes in archeology, and he loves puzzles. He is a self-proclaimed British gentleman, and “That’s what a gentleman does” is his favorite motto.

Luke… Full name Luke Triton. An English boy who respects Layton and calls himself “Layton’s number one apprentice.”

Emmy… Full name Emmy Altava. A beautiful woman of Asian lineage. She is Layton’s assistant, helping Layton with data and coming to help in times of trouble.

Janice… Full name Janice Quatlane. Famous opera singer. She used to be Layton’s student.

Oswald Whistler… One of the richest men in London. It seems that he was a famous pianist ten years ago.

Melina Whistler… The only daughter of the Whistler family. She was under medical treatment, but she died a year ago as her illness worsened. She was 22 years old.
