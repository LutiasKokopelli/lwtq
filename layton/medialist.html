Professor Layton and the London Holiday
レイトン教授と
ロンドンの休暇		 DS Game21/09/2007100%100%100%10% Either buy the Japanese version of Diabolical/Pandora’s Box or find its Rom online
Raw Text Transcript & Translation🇯🇵 🇬🇧		 London Holiday is a Japan-exclusive prologue to Diabolical Box 🇺🇸 / Pandora’s Box 🇪🇺. The DS version was included as a demo in the promotional Level-5 Premium Silver collection, which was given away at Tokyo Game Show 2007. The distribution of the Premium Silver software caused the Level-5 booth at the Tokyo Game Show to be full, making distribution impossible. Due to a flood of requests and opinions from users who were unable to obtain the software and wanted to experience Professor Layton and the London Holiday, this story was included in Professor Layton and the Diabolical Box 🇺🇸 / Pandora’s Box 🇪🇺.
